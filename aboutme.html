Who am I?

Hey everyone! I'm so glad to have you all here on my site! I'm Nicole Viray from Northern California. I'm a barista at my local Starbucks and absolutely love my crew there - we always have so much fun! Outside of work, you'll often find me at the gym, at the movie theatre, or at a local coffee shop. I also enjoy going to concerts! I'm huge on music; I listen to a little bit of everything, but metal and some girly pop are my faves! Fun fact: I'm a HUGE Metallica fan; I met James Hetfield's son and even Rob Trujillo's son [he's also the bassist for Suicidal Tendencies and Ottto!]. I'm also a huge nerd and love Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, and Stranger Things!

I hope you all enjoy my site! I've created this to give the guidance that I wish I had when I started my fitness journey. Fitness is a passion of mine; now I want to help others with their's! I want to show you all that it is very easy, you just need to take that first step.
